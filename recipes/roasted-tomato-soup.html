Roasted Tomato Soup

Ingredient list

  • 1.5kg of roma or plum tomatoes, cut in half
  • 8 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • Freshly ground salt and pepper
  • 0.5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 0.5 cup packed basil leaves
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1-2 cups water or vegetarian broth, depending on how thick you want the soup

How to make it, preparing the ingredients!

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Place halved tomatoes and garlic cloves on the baking sheet and drizzle with 3 tablespoons of olive oil.
  3. Generously season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for 40-45 minutes.
  4. Add 1/2 tablespoon olive oil to a large pot and place over medium heat.
  5. Add the onion slices and stir to coat the onions with olive oil.
  6. Check onions every 5-10 minutes until they have completely caramelized and turned golden in color. Around 20 minutes

How to make it, cooking time!

  1. Once tomatoes and garlic are done roasting, allow them to cool for 10 minutes, then add them to a food processor or high powered blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Add basil and caramelized onions and blend again.
  3. After blending, transfer back to pot, turn to medium low heat and add in oregano, vegetarian broth and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Allow tomato soup to simmer 10 minutes before serving.
